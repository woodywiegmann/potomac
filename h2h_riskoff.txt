ASSUMPTION NOTE
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CRDBX column uses SGOV daily returns as a proxy for money market
income. CRDBX's NAV is reported to two decimal places (~$14/share),
so daily money market accrual (~$0.003/share at 5% annual) is below
the rounding threshold and appears as 0.0000% on any single day.
Imputing SGOV's actual daily return gives a fair baseline that
reflects the ~5% annualized yield the fund is earning on its cash
position, net of SGOV's 0.09% expense ratio.

CAOS and DBMF returns are actual daily NAV total returns from Yahoo
Finance, net of each fund's own expense ratio (CAOS 0.63%, DBMF
0.85%). No additional fee adjustment is applied.
